Welcome to the preview page of Lazy Reflex Complex!


On this page you will find the upcoming music of the Lazy Reflex Complex project. The music presented here is unreleased and is available for previewing purposes only. Please do not share the link of this page.

All music from the LRCXX catalog will be released on 12" vinyl format and digital format. All tracks are mastered by Audiosculpture and fabricated by Record Industry.

The LRC project will also focus on other alternative electronic music styles, such as ambient its affiliates. This will be featured in the LRC's Ambient Series (LRCAMBIENTXX). The Ambient Series will be released on digital format and tape format.

The Lazy Reflex Complex project will launch very soon.. Please stay tuned!
